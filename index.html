Web and Software Development

Design, write, install, and maintain a variety of software applications for the web and mobile

The CIT Web and Software development concentration builds on the foundations in the Computer and Information Technology program and prepares students for careers as programmers, systems analysts, website designers, mobile application developers, and cloud computing engineers. Students learn how to create cutting-edge software applications, manage projects, collaborate with their peers, generate and exchange data, and successfully interact with users. Our graduates are highly sought in industries from IT, healthcare, insurance, and retail, which is evidenced by our high placement rate and consistent ranking in starting salaries for IUPUI graduates.
